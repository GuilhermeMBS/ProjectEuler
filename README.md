# 🧮 Project Euler Solutions

Welcome to my repository of solutions for [Project Euler](https://projecteuler.net/)! 

This repository serves as an archive of my journey through these mathematical and programming challenges, as well as an educational resource for others who are learning algorithmic problem-solving.

---

## 📜 Project Euler Sharing Policy (The "First 100" Rule)

You will notice that this repository **only contains solutions for the first 100 problems**. 

Project Euler has a strict policy regarding the public sharing of solutions to maintain the integrity of their platform. However, they make a specific exception for the earlier problems to foster learning and discussion. As stated in their official guidelines:

> *"However, the rule about sharing solutions outside of Project Euler does not apply to the first one-hundred problems, as long as any discussion clearly aims to instruct methods, not just provide answers, and does not directly threaten to undermine the enjoyment of solving later problems. Problems 1 to 100 provide a wealth of helpful introductory teaching material and if you are able to respect our requirements, then we give permission for those problems and their solutions to be discussed elsewhere."*

In full compliance with this policy, I will not be uploading or sharing any solutions past Problem 100.

## 📂 Repository Structure & Methodology

To align with the platform's goal of *instructing methods* rather than just providing copy-paste code, this repository is structured to explain my thought process behind every answer.

* Each problem has its own dedicated folder.
* Inside each folder, alongside the source code, you will find a **`.txt` file**. 
* I use these text files to discuss my ideas, break down the mathematical concepts involved, and explain the algorithmic approach I took to arrive at the solution.

### Example Structure:
```text
/Project-Euler
  ├── /FirstQuarter
  │     └── ProblemZero
  │           ├── ProblemZero.cpp
  │           └── ProblemZero.txt    <-- (Read this for the methodology!)
  │     └── ProblemOne.cpp
  │           ├── ProblemOne.cpp
  │           └── ProblemOne.txt
  │     ...
  ├── /SecondQuarter
  │     ...
```
